Members

Mato Durinda

Singer, guitarist, piano player and founder of Tublatanka
* November 8, 1961

Martin "Maťo" Ďurinda is the vocalist, guitarist and composer of the Slovak hard rock/heavy metal band Tublatanka. He is self-taught in playing the guitar and piano. He was taught to sing by his mother, who was a voice teacher at the university. While studying at the university, Maťo Ďurinda met Ďuro Černý and Palo Horváth, with whom they founded a rock band called "Tublat" after the name of the negative ape character from the novel Tarzan, which was later changed to the name Tublatanka used to this day.

Juraj Topor

Bass guitarist
* May 22, 1965

Juraj "Ďuro" Topor is the current bass guitarist of the Slovak hard rock band Tublatanka. He comes from Topolčany, where he first founded his own band, and has been with Tublatanka since 1994, initially as a security guard and technician. In 1995, when bass guitarist Paľo Horváth left Tublatanka, Topor successfully passed the audition and has been in the band as bass guitarist ever since.

Peter Schloser

Drummer
* January 17, 1973

Peter "Peťo" Schlosser is a Slovak heavy metal drummer. He was in the death metal band Thalarion until 2004, and since then he has been the drummer in the hard rock band Tublatanka. He joined Tublatanka as a replacement for drummer Martin Uherčík, who left the band for family reasons.

Past Members

Pavol Horvath

Bass guitarist
* March 14, 1963

Pavol "Paľo" Horvath is a Slovak bass guitarist. He is known from the Slovak hard rock band Tublatanka, in which he has been active since 1982. He recorded five studio albums with Tublatanka. He left the band in 1993. His high-pitched vocals are typical for many Tublatanka songs such as "Prometeus" or "Stojím, padám", in which Paľo Horváth was the lead singer.

Juraj Cerny

Drummer
* July 10, 1961 - † February 26, 2016

Juraj "Ďuro" Černý was a Slovak drummer who became famous mainly as the drummer of the hard rock band Tublatanka, which he founded in 1982 together with Martin Ďurinda. During his 10 years with Tublatanka, he recorded 7 studio albums. He left the band in 1996. In 1995, he became the author of the Ďuro Černý Company project, which brought together several stars of the domestic music scene. For twenty years he struggled with a rare intestinal disease, which ultimately led to his premature death. He died on February 26, 2016. The cause of death was perforation of the colon.

Jozef Duban

Singer and guitarist
* June 7, 1964 - † April 25, 2003

Jozef "Dodo" Dubán was the singer and guitarist of the Tublatanka band. Since Maťo Ďurinda could not find a replacement for the departing Paľo Horváth, he approached Dodo Dubán. He accepted the offer and joined Tublatanka. In the new line-up of the popular hard-rock band, Dubán played backing guitar and also co-wrote some songs. In 1995, they recorded the album Znovuzrodenie. Dodo Dubán recorded his second and last studio album with Tublatanka in 2001, titled Pánska jazda. After his girlfriend Jana Šomóšiová, with whom he had lived for eight years, left him, Dodo Dubán suffered from depression. He jumped out of the window of his seventh-floor apartment. That day, he and Tublatanka were supposed to christen the selection album Zlatá Tublatanka 20 rockov.

Martin Uhercik

Drummer
* October 16, 1969

Martin "Malý Peci" Uherčík was member of hard rock band Tublatanka from 1995 to 2001. He replaced Juraj Cerny on drums who left Tublatanka.

February 2025

Tublatanka Tribute 1985-1992

November 2024

Opus Publishing has prepared a unique 5CD box set TUBLATANKA – TRIBUTE (1985 – 1992), which contains the first five albums of the now legendary rock legend, the band Tublatanka. The albums Tublatanka, Skúsime to cez vesmír, Žeravé znamenie osodu, Nebo-Peklo-Raj and Volanie divočiny are full of now cult hits, many of which will forever be part of our national cultural heritage. Moreover, these are albums that the band recorded in its original iconic line-up of Ďurinda-Horváth-Černý. In total, the set contains 53 songs.

Ďurinda among award-winning personalities of the music scene

September 2024

Seven personalities of the Slovak music scene were honored by the Music Fund on Tuesday afternoon at a ceremony in Bratislava's Zichy Palace.

Martin Ďurinda, singer, composer and musician, leader of the band Tublatanka, received the Gejza Dusík Award for 40 years on the Slovak music scene and for organizing a large Czech-Slovak tour on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the band Tublatanka. Ďurinda was nominated for the award by the Association of Authors and Performers of Popular Music.

Souboj Rebelů Tour

Czech Republic, September 2019

After a spectacular success, Tublatanka and Citron are leaving for the second part of the Souboj Rebelů tour in the Czech Republic!
After a year, the Czechoslovak music legends are going on a joint tour of the Czech Republic called Souboj Rebelů Tour 2019! In the spring of this year, the rock bands played an extremely successful tour, on which they sold out eleven halls in the Czech Republic, and therefore they will meet again to continue this phenomenal success!
Don't miss this honest rock blast full of energy.
On the spring tour, a joint composition was created - a duet by Maťo Ďurinda and Láďa Křížek "Strong and unbreakable" about the Czech Republic and Slovakia and the friendship between the two states, which was played at the end of each concert and the fans will also hear it in the second part of the tour.
At the Souboj Rebelů Tour 2019, fans can expect a new, monstrous scene full of great effects and new video animations. Citron will present and offer a new single, which will be a harbinger of a new studio album.
At the same time, fans will be able to buy the new mini DVD Tublatanka / Citron, which was filmed this year in Prague's Malá Sportovní hall.
You have something to look forward to again!.
